ABOUT

Beautiful Spaces

Philippe loves creating beautiful spaces. For the better part of his life he learned how to create and design warm and welcoming interiors and exteriors in his native France as well as abroad.

Using centuries' old natural European materials and techniques, he brings walls to artistic life in color and texture with limestone, marble and earth pigments.

Contemporary and old-world styles collide with grace, charm and simple elegance inspired by the Provence countryside. Surround yourself by beauty, originality, and natural style. We welcome the opportunity to help you create your dream home ambiance.
